Carrot and Chutney Sandwiches

Why This:

Carrots are packed with nutrients and are pocket-friendly too. Here we have a very simple carrot and chutney sandwich recipe that your little one will be able to make and enjoy any time.

Ingredients

  • One cup of grated carrots
  • Four tablespoons of green chutney
  • Twelve slices of whole wheat bread
  • Two tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • One cup grated paneer
  • Salt

How To Make:

  1. In a bowl, take the grated carrot, add mayonnaise, salt, and mix well.
  2. Add grated paneer, green chutney, and salt to taste in another bowl and mix we ll.
  3. One sandwich will require three slices of bread. Take one slice of bread and spread the carrot chutney mixture.
  4. Cover it with another slice of bread and then spread the green chutney and paneer mixture on that slice, and finally place the last slice of bread on top.
  5. Trim off the crusts and enjoy.
